📝 **Procedure**
1. Log in or register an account for your accommodation at [https://kbtt.xuatnhapcanh.gov.vn](https://kbtt.xuatnhapcanh.gov.vn).
2. Choose the function "Khai báo tạm trú cho người nước ngoài" (Declare temporary residence for foreigners).
3. Enter the guest’s passport details manually or by scanning / OCR.
4. Fill in the arrival date, planned departure date, and room / place of stay.
5. Save and submit. You can track the declaration status on the system.

⏱ **Time limit:** You must declare **within 12 hours** of the guest’s arrival (24 hours if your area qualifies as remote / remote countryside).
