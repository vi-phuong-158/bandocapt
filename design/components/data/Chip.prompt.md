Fully-rounded pill. The emerald `nearby` chip is the distance tag on result cards; `glass` makes a frosted tag that floats over the detail photo.

```jsx
<Chip tone="nearby" icon="near_me">1.2 km</Chip>
<Chip glass icon="near_me">1.2 km</Chip>
```

Use `Badge` (square radius) for category labels; `Chip` (pill radius) for distances and source links.
